About Us

Welcome to our platform! At ArchyType, we are dedicated to helping architecture students refine and showcase their portfolios. Whether you are just starting your journey or preparing for your next big review, our platform provides the tools and resources you need to present your work in the most professional way possible. ArchyType is designed to streamline the portfolio-building process, offering easy-to-use templates, feedback tools, and organizational features to help you stay on top of your progress. We understand that a well-curated portfolio is essential for your academic and professional growth, and we're here to support you every step of the way. With ArchyType, you can showcase your creativity, technical skills, and design evolution in a visually appealing and organized manner. Get ready to elevate your architectural portfolio and make your work stand out in the world of design!



How we started

Nistha, Chevanique , Lucero, and Jose, four architectural students, recognized early in 2024 that they were all having trouble with portfolio reviews. They were dissatisfied with the dearth of easily available materials and comments, so they set out to develop a website that would assist other students in improving their portfolios. Using their combined expertise in outreach, coding, and design, they put in many hours to create an approachable platform where students could submit their work and get helpful feedback from industry experts and peers. Following several months of working together, they were able to successfully launch the website in September 2023, which attracted an increasing number of architecture students eager to improve their portfolios and encourage one another on their artistic endeavors. Their initiative not only filled a significant gap in architectural education but also fostered a collaborative spirit among aspiring architects.
